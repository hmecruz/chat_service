Chats (Chat Groups):
_id: MongoDB's default _id field (or a custom chat_id).
users: An array of user_ids representing the members of the chat.
createdAt: Timestamp of when the chat was created.
groupName: Name of the chat group.

Messages:
_id: MongoDB's default _id field (or a custom message_id).
chat_id: Reference to the _id of the Chats collection.
sender_id: Identifier of the message sender.
sentAt: Timestamp of when the message was sent.
content: The message content.
editedAt: Timestamp of when the message was edited (optional).
deletedAt: Timestamp of when the message was deleted (optional).
